a. Below 25 - F
b. 25 to 45 - E
c. 45 to 50 - D
d. 50 to 60 - C

f. Above 80 - A

---

Sample input 1
60

---

Sample output 1
B

---

Sample input 2
98.5

---

Sample output 2
A

---

Purpose
any student can enter their marks per subject and check their grades easily.
